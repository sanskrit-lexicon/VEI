About this edition

This is the digital home of the The Vedic Index of Names and Subjects (VEI) within the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon (CDSL), a volunteer project that digitises, corrects, and openly publishes the foundational Sanskrit dictionaries as citable, reproducible data. Corrections are tracked as change files in this repository; the searchable text is served on the Cologne site.
